Normalized predicted geo indicator by LLMs

Absolute Income prediction

The absolute predicted average income per habitat for a region (denoted as I_{\text{region/LLM}}) against the ground truth average income per habitat from Eurostat (denoted as I_{\text{region/Eurostat}}).

Relative Income prediction

The relative predicted relative income (RI_{\text{LLM}}) against the relative difference between the ground truth regional income and the ground truth national income, both obtained from Eurostat.

Definitions

  • I_{\text{region/LLM}}: Average income per habitat for a region predicted by the LLM.
  • I_{\text{region/Eurostat}}: Ground truth average income per habitat for a region from Eurostat.
  • I_{\text{country/Eurostat}}: Ground truth average income per habitat for a country from Eurostat.
  • RI_{\text{LLM}}: Relative income predicted by the LLM.
