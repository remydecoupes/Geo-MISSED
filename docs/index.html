Definitions

Income (\( I\)) Relative Icome (\( RI\)) Difference (\( Diff\)) Normalized Difference (\( NormDiff\))
\( I_{ ext{region/LLM}} \): Average income per habitat for a region predicted by the LLM. \( RI_{ ext{LLM}} \): Relative income predicted by the LLM. \( Diff_{ ext{I}} \): \( I_{ ext{region/Eurostat}} \) - \( I_{ ext{region/LLM}} \) \( NormDiff_{ ext{I}} \): (\( I_{ ext{region/Eurostat}} \) - \( I_{ ext{region/LLM}} \)) / \( I_{ ext{country/Eurostat}} \)
\( I_{ ext{region/Eurostat}} \): Ground truth average income per habitat for a region from Eurostat. \( RI_{ ext{Eurostat}} \): \( I_{ ext{region/Eurostat}} \) - \( I_{ ext{country/Eurostat}} \) \( Diff_{ ext{RI}} \): \( RI_{ ext{LLM}} \) - \( RI_{ ext{Eurostat}} \) \( NormDiff_{ ext{RI}} \): (\( RI_{ ext{LLM}} \) - \( RI_{ ext{Eurostat}} \)) / \( I_{ ext{country/Eurostat}} \)
\( I_{ ext{country/Eurostat}} \): Ground truth average income per habitat for a country from Eurostat.
